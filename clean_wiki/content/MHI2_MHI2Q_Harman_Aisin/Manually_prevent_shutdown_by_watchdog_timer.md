# Manually prevent shutdown by watchdog timer


> [!INFO]
> When you run [Emergency IFS](https://mibwiki.one/doc/how-to-boot-ifs-emergencyifs-start-blue-emergency-efu-u6Pt9h5acV) without [ignition emulator](https://mibwiki.one/doc/simulate-ignition-KOw2FI2dTD), and you did not enable [DeveloperMode](https://mibwiki.one/doc/how-to-boot-ifs-emergencyifs-start-blue-emergency-efu-u6Pt9h5acV), the watchdog timer automatically shuts the MIB down in 60 seconds.
To prevent the shutdown, press DVD eject button every 30-50 seconds:
