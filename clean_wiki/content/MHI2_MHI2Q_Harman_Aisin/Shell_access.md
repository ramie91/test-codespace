# Shell access

## RCC Shell login

 ![](assets/80acdb45-4801-46db-9db6-9d8cb34f2f6b.redirect_id_80acdb45-4801-46db-9db6-9d8cb34f2f6b)

To login, type “root” and press ENTER to confirm

 ![](assets/50bd6f94-8ba5-4071-bd6c-59611ce420bf.redirect_id_50bd6f94-8ba5-4071-bd6c-59611ce420bf)

Enter password matching your FW (input will NOT be displayed) and press ENTER to confirm

 ![](assets/95bb3530-416f-4e62-9f14-a7bbc45698fb.redirect_id_95bb3530-416f-4e62-9f14-a7bbc45698fb)


> [!INFO]
> RCC shell can be identified by QNX Neutrino (rcc) banner and **/ >** console prompt
