# Copy screenshots to SD

The screenshots are saved on the hard disk of the MMI unit. To copy them to SD1, open GEM got there to:

```
production>debugging_prod>Copy MMX / RCC CoF, ErL & ScS (only AU) to SD1
```

Wait until "Copying files to ... done". Look for the screenshots and log files on the`SD1` in the folder like`01_Export_20160913_115634.`
