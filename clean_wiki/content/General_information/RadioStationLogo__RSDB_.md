## Restore missing logos after firmware update


1. Turn your MIB on. Wait for it to load.
2. Go to `RADIO`.
3. Select first radio preset.
4. Press and hold the finger on the first radio preset. Station will be freshly assigned to the preset and logo will be pulled from the database if available.
5. Repeat for each preset.
