## Activation of the hub ports in the **adaptations of block 5F** is the same for all MIBs:

* ENG248432-ENG248482-function_configuration_media-USB: **usb_ipod**

  (MIB2 has this switch in long coding, byte19)
* IDE10480-MAS10741-Interface for external media: activation-Connection 1: **activated**
* IDE10480-MAS10742-Interface for external media: activation-Connection 2: **activated**
