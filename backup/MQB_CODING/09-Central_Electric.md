# 09-Central Electric

# Turn completely Off Lights on Leon KL, Golf MK8 and so on …

Go to:


1. --> 09 - Central Electrics --> Adaptions
2. --> Go to "Licht_bedienung"
   Now Disable:
3. --> "LTM_BGL_Wegabhängig"
4. --> "LTM_BGL_Geschwindigkeitsabhängig"
5. --> "LTM_Global_OFF_Wegabhängig"
6. --> "LTM_Global_OFF_Geschwindigkeitsabhängig"
7. --> LTM_Global_OFF_Geschwindigkeitsabhängig Parameter"

\
You can now turn off the light at night (in some areas it is mandatory to turn off the light at night when a patrol asks you to stop for routine check)
