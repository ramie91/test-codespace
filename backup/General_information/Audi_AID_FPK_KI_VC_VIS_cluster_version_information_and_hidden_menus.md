## Audi A6 C8 version information


1. Switch the Ignition on
2. Set instrument cluster to trip data
3. On the steering wheel, keep holding for 10 seconds the button, situated on the left from the `OK wheel`.
4. Go to `Version information`.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xuzkiVJ41HU>

## Audi A3 8V Green Engineering Menu (GEM)


1. In adaptations of the block/STG/module `17`, enable developer mode
2. Turn the ignition on.
3. On the steering wheel, keep holding `”OK wheel”` and `”>”` buttons at the same time, until [GEM appears](https://mibwiki.one/doc/green-engineering-menu-NYy0Mqd9kA).

<https://youtu.be/z6uXa9qs_Ik>
