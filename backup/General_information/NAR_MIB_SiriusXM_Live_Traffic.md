# NAR MIB SiriusXM Live Traffic

• Coding - Using VCDS/ODIS/VCP/OBDEleven 

Enter 5F Infotainment \n Adaptations \n Fee-Based Traffic Information (TMC) Change Value to 34305

\
• Call (or chat) with SiriusXM Support (must have an active music subscription)

Request for SiriusXM Traffic and Travel Link Infotainment Features to be added to your Radio ID (your radio ID will be in the settings on your radio or under your SiriusXM radio list)

\
After the features have been added, send a refresh signal to your radio (https://www.siriusxm.com/refresh) and restart the MIB by holding the power button for 15 seconds, after the radio restarts, stay on a SiriusXM station until “Subscription has Changed” shows at the top of your radio screen.

\
DONE you now have live traffic without VW Car-Net, Audi Connect or Porsche Connect
