## RCC Shell login

 ![](/api/attachments.redirect?id=80acdb45-4801-46db-9db6-9d8cb34f2f6b)

To login, type “root” and press ENTER to confirm

 ![](/api/attachments.redirect?id=50bd6f94-8ba5-4071-bd6c-59611ce420bf)

RCC shell can be identified by QNX Neutrino (rcc) banner and **/ >** console prompt
